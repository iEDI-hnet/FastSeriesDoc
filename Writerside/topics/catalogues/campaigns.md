### Create the campaign
1. Go to the Catalogues > Campaigns and click the "Create a campaign" button.
2. Give the campaign an internal name, add a number as an internal identifier, provide campaign details, and define its duration.
3. Add multiple products grouped by category.
- In the category tree, do any of the following:
  * To include all products, click Select the checkbox of the parent category.
  * To include specific categories of products, select the checkbox of each category that you want to include.
  * To include or exclude an individual product, select or deselect the checkbox of product.
4. When complete, Click Save.
