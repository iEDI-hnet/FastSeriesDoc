# Customers
A customer is an entity, such as a company or organization, that buys your products or services. They can also be partners in the sense that the relationship is mutually beneficial.

> You can create multiple customers at once with a Customer import, or add customers individually.

### Create Customers

![%product%](customer.png) { width="700" }

For create Customers List you need:
1. **Locate the "Create Customers" option:** Find the customer creation page by navigating through the "Partners" menu. Go to Partners > Customers > Create Customer.
2. **Enter company information:** Input the customer's name, any tax or registration numbers (like ABN, GST, or EIN), and other identifiers.
3. **Add contact and address details:** Fill in the contact details, such as email and phone number, and provide one or more addresses. 
4. **Save the new customer:** Click "Save"  to finalize the entry.

Customers list can be used for creating customer specific pricing catalogue.
