# Suppliers

### Create Suppliers
A supplier is a company that provides goods or services to your business. A partnership can be a strategic collaboration beyond a simple transaction.

> You can create multiple suppliers at once with a Supplier import, or add suppliers individually.

![%product%](suppliers.png) { width="700" }

For create Suppliers List you need:
1. **Locate the "Create Supplier" option:** Find the supplier creation page by navigating through the "Partners" menu. Go to Partners > Suppliers > Create Supplier.
2. **Enter company information:** Input the supplier's name, any tax or registration numbers (like ABN, GST, or EIN), and other identifiers.
3. **Add contact and address details:** Fill in the contact details, such as email and phone number, and provide one or more addresses.
4. **Save the new supplier:** Click "Save"  to finalize the entry.
