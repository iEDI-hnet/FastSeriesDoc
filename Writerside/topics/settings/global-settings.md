# Settings

## Overview

Company Settings manage your organization's information, branding, and business rules. These settings are shared across your organization and appear in PDF documents, product cards and catalogues.

> For personal user preferences such as display options, notifications, and security settings, see the [User Settings](user-settings.md) documentation.{style="note"}

## Company Settings

### Company Information

The Company Information section allows you to maintain your business details that will be used throughout the application and in generated documents. Complete company information ensures professional presentation in all your business materials.

**Required Fields:** The system tracks completion of your company information. A badge indicator shows how many required fields are still missing (out of 7 total fields).

**Company Information Fields:**
- **Company Name:** Your business or organization name
- **Organization Number:** Your VAT, KVK, CVR, ORG number, or similar business registration number
- **Street Name/Number:** Your business street address
- **Zip Code/City:** Your business postal code and city
- **Country:** Your business country
- **Contact Name:** Primary contact person's name
- **Contact Email:** Primary contact email address
- **Contact Phone:** Primary contact phone number

**How to Update:** Navigate to Settings → Company Information. Fill in the required fields and click Save. The system will validate your input and show any missing required information.

### Logo

Add your business logo to appear in PDF documents including product cards and catalogues. This ensures consistent branding across all your business materials.

**Logo Requirements:**
- Supported formats: Common image formats (PNG, JPG, SVG)
- Recommended size: High resolution for best quality in PDF documents
- The logo will be automatically resized to fit document layouts

**How to Add/Update Logo:**
1. Navigate to Settings → Company Information
2. Click on the logo area or upload button
3. Select your logo image file
4. The logo will be uploaded and displayed immediately
5. Click Save to persist the changes

> The logo appears in all PDF exports, so ensure it represents your brand professionally.{style="note"}

### Homepage URL

Add your business homepage or other important links to be displayed in PDF documents (product cards, catalogues). These link help recipients find more information about your business.

**How to Add Links:**
1. Navigate to Settings → Company Information
2. In the "My Homepage" section, enter your URL
3. Click Save to persist all changes

**Link Format:** Always use full URLs starting with http:// or https:// (e.g., https://your_organization.com)

**Display:** Saved link will appear in your PDF documents, making it easy for partners and customers to find your online presence.

### Sharing Quality Level Rules

Configure quality level rules that define what partners need to deliver for your webshop. These rules help ensure consistent product data quality across your business network.

**Purpose:** Quality level rules specify the minimum requirements for product information that partners must provide when sharing catalogues with you. This helps maintain data standards and reduces manual data cleanup.

**How to Configure:**
1. Navigate to Settings → Sharing Quality Level Rules
2. Review and adjust the rules according to your business needs
3. You can configure rules at different levels (general and product-specific)
4. Click Save to apply your rules

**Product Quality Level Rules:** For more granular control, you can configure product-specific quality rules that apply to individual products or product categories.

### Pause Synchronization

Temporarily pause FastSync from synchronizing your shared catalogues. This is useful when you need to make bulk changes or when you want to prevent automatic updates during maintenance periods.

**When to Use:**
- During bulk product updates or imports
- When performing system maintenance
- When you want to control when synchronization occurs

**How to Pause:**
1. Navigate to Settings
2. Find "Pause Synchronization"
3. Toggle the switch to pause synchronization
4. A warning message will appear confirming that FastSync is paused

> When synchronization is paused, changes made by partners won't be automatically synchronized. Remember to resume synchronization when you're ready to receive updates again.{style="warning"}

### Billing History

Access your billing history and invoices from the Microsoft Store. This section provides a centralized location to view and manage your subscription and payment information.

**How to Access:**
1. Navigate to Settings
2. Click on "Billing history"
3. View your invoices and billing information

**Billing Information:** You can also update your billing information directly from this section to ensure accurate invoicing and payment processing.
